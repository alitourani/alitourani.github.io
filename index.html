About Me

Researcher? Developer? Both!

I am an R&D Specialist and a flexible Senior Software Engineer with 8+ years of experience in practical computer vision and AI system design and deployment. Currently, I pursue a Postdoctoral Research Associate position at the University of Luxembourg, developing vision-language models and generative AI solutions for real-world robotic applications. I have a proven track record in open-source software development and building research-driven, most of which are available on my GitHub profile. I am passionate about advancing the fields of robotics and Gen-AI through innovative research and collaboration.
