[Project] E-Ticketing for Medical Clinics

January - August 2013

Target Client/End-User: Guilan University of Medical Sciences

Affiliation: B.Sc. final project at the University of Guilan

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): implements three distinct, secure access levels (Patient, Nurse, and Doctor) to provide user interface and data permissions specifically to each workflow.
  • Automated Notification: simple integration with email services to deliver real-time appointment confirmations, cancellations, and reminders.
  • Simplified Clinic Workflow: digitizes the end-to-end appointment scheduling lifecycle, reducing administrative overhead and decreasing patient wait times.
  • Responsive Interface: features an accessible layout to allow medical professionals and patients navigate scheduling effortlessly.
  • 💡 Stack: Php, Oracle Database, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
