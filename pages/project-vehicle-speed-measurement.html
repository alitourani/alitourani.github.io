[Project] Smart Cities: Vehicle Speed Measurement

November 2016 - December 2018

Target End-User: Municipalities, Traffic Law Enforcement, & Urban Planning Authorities

Affiliation: M.Sc. Thesis Project (University of Guilan), Rasht, Iran

Project Ecosystem: GitHub | Paper#1 | Paper#2 | Demo Video

  • Automated Velocity Estimation: utilizes advanced video stream analytics to track passing vehicles dynamically and calculate their real-time traveling speeds.
  • Intelligent Violation Logging: programmed to instantly flag vehicles exceeding predefined local speed limits, capturing and storing data for regulatory compliance.
  • Enterprise Municipal Scalability: engineered with a modular framework for integration into existing city wide traffic camera networks.
  • Commercial Grounding & Peer Review: transitioned from an academic Master's thesis into a commercially viable business incubation proposal at GSTP.
  • 💡 Stack: C#, OpenCV, EmguCV, AForge.NET, SQL Server
