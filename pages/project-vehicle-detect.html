[Project] Vehicle Detection & Tracking

September 2018 - March 2019

Target Audience: Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) & Smart City Authorities, Rasht, Iran

Affiliation: M.Sc. Research Project (University of Guilan) & Post-Graduation Grant (GSTP Funded)

Project Ecosystem: GitHub | Paper#1

  • Robust Vehicle Detection: using Faster R-CNN to achieve accurate localized bounding boxes for multi-class vehicles.
  • Accurate Feature Extraction: isolates intricate visual features of targets directly from roadway camera streams, with precise tracking across varied environmental conditions.
  • Funded Commercial Validation: recognized for its scientific robustness and real-world applicability, earning competitive research backing from the Guilan Science and Technology Park (GSTP).
  • 💡 Stack: Python, MATLAB
